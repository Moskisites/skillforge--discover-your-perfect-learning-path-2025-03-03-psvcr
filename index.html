About SkillForge

Founded in 2020 by a team of education technology experts and industry professionals, SkillForge emerged from a simple observation: finding the right online courses shouldn't be overwhelming. Our founders experienced firsthand the challenges of navigating the vast landscape of online learning while trying to upskill their own teams. This inspired them to create a platform that would make professional development more accessible and effective for everyone.

At SkillForge, our mission is to empower individuals to take control of their professional growth through personalized learning experiences. We believe in the power of education to transform lives and are committed to helping our users make informed decisions about their learning journey. Our core values of transparency, innovation, and user-centered design drive everything we do, from our course recommendations to our price alert system.
